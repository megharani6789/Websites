WHAT IS HEALTH ?

In 1948, the World Health Organization (WHO) constitution states :

“Health is a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease and infirmity(physical or mental weakness.)”.

WHAT IS MENTAL HEALTH ?



World Health Organization (WHO) definition of health as "a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity"

MORE DETAILS.....

  • Mental health can be influenced by a variety of factors, including genetics, environment, and life experiences. Some common risk factors for mental health problems include childhood trauma, substance abuse, chronic stress, and social isolation.
  • There are a number of effective treatments for mental health conditions, including therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes such as exercise and stress reduction techniques. It's important to seek professional help if you or someone you know is experiencing symptoms of a mental health condition.
  • It's also important to remember that mental health is just as important as physical health, and taking care of your mental health can have a positive impact on your overall well-being. This can include practices such as self-care, social support, and seeking help when needed.
